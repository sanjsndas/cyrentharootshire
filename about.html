Our mission

To cultivate a library of practical, evidence‑informed gardening knowledge — without the noise. We believe in soil, patience, and the long view.

Every article is grounded in hands‑on trials, peer‑reviewed research, and the wisdom of traditional practices. We garden in zone 6b, but our insights travel.

What we stand for

  • 🧪 Curiosity over certainty — we update as we learn
  • 📚 Deep content — 1500+ word guides, not listicles
