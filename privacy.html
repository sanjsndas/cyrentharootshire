Privacy Policy

Your trust is the root of our garden. This policy explains how we care for your personal data.

🌱 Our commitment

At Rootshire Pavilion, we believe in transparency and simplicity. We collect only what is necessary to tend to your inquiries, event registrations, and garden correspondence. This privacy policy was last reviewed in April 2026.

We never sell or rent your personal information. Any data you share is used solely to enrich your experience with our grounds and community.

📋 What we collect

  • Contact details — name, email address, telephone number (if you provide it).
  • Correspondence — messages, inquiries, and notes you send via our contact form or direct email.
  • Event preferences — workshops, garden tours, or talks you express interest in.
  • Technical data — anonymised IP address, browser type, and referral page for analytics (see our Cookie Policy).

🔒 How we use your data

  • Response — to reply to your messages and provide the information you request.
  • Booking — to confirm your place at events and send practical details.
  • Improvement — to understand which parts of our website and gardens interest you most.
  • Legal — to comply with any applicable legal obligations (e.g., records retention).

📦 Data storage & security

Your data is stored on secure servers located in the United Kingdom. We use industry-standard encryption and access controls. Only essential team members have access, and they are trained in data stewardship.

We keep your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above, or as required by law. If you wish to have your data removed, please contact us — we will respond promptly.

🤝 Your rights

  • Access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — you may update or correct your information at any time.
  • Erasure — you can ask us to delete your data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Objection — you can object to certain uses of your data (e.g., direct marketing).

To exercise any of these rights, simply write to us at privacy@rootshirepavilion.co.uk or send a letter to our Wold Lane address.

🍃 Third‑party services

We use minimal third‑party tools to keep our website running and to understand visitor patterns. These include:

  • Analytics — privacy‑friendly, aggregated statistics (no personal data shared).
  • Email — we use a secure email service to manage correspondence; your messages are never stored elsewhere.

We do not integrate social media plugins that track you without consent. Any external links are clearly marked and governed by their own policies.
