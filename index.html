    Frequently asked questions

    How often should I water raised beds?

    Deep watering 2–3 times per week in summer, adjusting for rainfall and mulch. Use a moisture meter or finger test.

    What is the best mulch for vegetable gardens?

    Compost‑based mulch or straw. Avoid fresh wood chips near annual vegetables — they can tie up nitrogen.

    When to start seeds indoors?

    6–8 weeks before last frost for tomatoes, peppers; 4–6 weeks for brassicas. Check our seed starting calendar.
