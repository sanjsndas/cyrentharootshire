Cookies Policy

We use minimal, respectful cookies to make your visit to our digital garden as pleasant as a stroll through the grounds.

🍪 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our pages, and improve your experience. At Rootshire Pavilion, we use only essential and performance cookies — no intrusive tracking.

This policy explains which cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences. Last updated: April 2026.

📋 Types of cookies we use

  • Essential — necessary for basic site functionality (e.g., form submissions, session management). These are set automatically and cannot be turned off.
  • Analytics — privacy‑friendly statistics that help us understand which pages are most popular. We use aggregated data only, with no personal identification.
  • Preferences — remember your choices, such as language or region, if you set them.

🔍 List of cookies

  • rootshire_session — essential: maintains your session while you browse. Expires after 30 minutes.
  • rootshire_pref — stores your preference for dark/light mode (if you choose one). Lasts for 6 months.
  • _ga_root — analytics: used by our privacy‑first analytics tool to count visitors. No personal data is stored.
  • rootshire_consent — remembers your cookie consent choice. Expires after 1 year.

We do not use advertising, tracking, or third‑party cookies from social media platforms. Your data stays within our garden.

⚙️ Manage your preferences

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, but please note that some site features may not work as intended.

  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox — Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

Alternatively, you can change your consent at any time by clicking the “Cookie settings” link in our footer.

🌱 Your consent

When you first visit our site, we ask for your permission to set non‑essential cookies. You can accept or decline, and you can change your mind later. We respect your choice.

  • Accept all — enables analytics and preference cookies for a smoother experience.
  • Decline — only essential cookies are set; the site still works perfectly.

We never use cookies for marketing or profiling. Our philosophy is simple: less data, more trust.
